WHO IS Dr ALKA CHADHA

The Water Alchemist: Transform Chaos into Calm, Clarity, and Flow

Dr ALKA CHADHA

Dr. Alka Chadha is a global keynote speaker and a practicing doctor for the last 30 years. Through the lens of Medical Science, Yoga and Meditation, NLP practices, and the powerful Wisdom of Water, she shows individuals how they can build adaptability, resilience, and playfulness. She blends the thought leadership of the East and the innovation of the West to help organizations build happy, harmonious workplaces, unlocking greater productivity and prosperity. Dr. Alka has spoken at international platforms including PSS 2024, MAPS Kuala Lumpur, and the International Congress on Diabetes. Today, Dr Alka serves as President of PSAI Mumbai Chapter (2025–26), and has been the Editor of the Newsletter of PSAI for the past 3 years. Dr Alka has actively participated in the APSS (past 3 years), GSS, and has been the core of PSS India for 4 years. She has hosted 80+ LinkedIn Live episodes on the Extraordinary Power of the Mind. Founder of her ‘Endocrine Centre,’ her ‘Happiness School,’ and ‘Seniors Club’, She’s the author of four transformational books. She shares her story of personal transformation, turning life’s challenges into purposeful action and lasting impact. You will walk away with practical tools to take action, with clarity, purpose, and the heart.
